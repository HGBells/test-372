Game Information

Welcome to the 372 Pages Incremental Game! Your goal is to accumulate "372 Pages" by reading the books reviewed on the podcast. Each book generates a unique resource, which you can convert into "372 Pages".

You start with 372 clicks. They regenerate automatically at a base rate of 1 click every 4 seconds, up to a maximum of 372 clicks. Use them wisely!

Click a book's "Read" button to generate its specific resource. When you have enough, click "Convert" to gain "372 Pages".

Spend "372 Pages" on upgrades to boost your reading efficiency and passive income!
